About Me_

Hi, my name is Stefan and I am a Unity developer.
I was introduced to programming at a young age, when I learned to code games on the graphing calculator of my older brother. Programming has since become my passion and I really enjoy solving hard problems with coding.

Besides programming computers I like tinkering with electronics in my free time.
